Welcome to Gid's Guitars!

About Gid's Guitars For nearly 50 years, Gid's Guitars has been the go-to destination for musicians seeking high-quality guitars and exceptional service. Established in 1975, we have built a strong reputation as a trusted, independent retailer dedicated to providing the best instruments and expertise to our community. Whether you're a seasoned professional, an aspiring musician, or just someone looking to pick up the guitar for the first time, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Story

Gid's Guitars was founded on the belief that every musician deserves access to premium instruments and knowledgeable service. Our journey began in a small shop filled with a carefully curated selection of guitars, amps, and accessories, and over the years, we have grown into a beloved institution within the local music scene. Despite our expansion, we have stayed true to our roots—offering personalized customer care and a welcoming environment where musicians of all skill levels can find inspiration.

Our Commitment

At Gid’s Guitars, we take pride in offering an unmatched selection of top-tier instruments, including electric, acoustic, and bass guitars from the industry’s most respected brands. But we don’t just sell guitars—we help musicians find the perfect instrument that fits their style, sound, and budget. Our team of passionate experts is always available to provide hands-on guidance, answer questions, and share their deep knowledge of tonewoods, pickups, and playability. We also believe in building lasting relationships with our customers. That’s why we offer professional guitar setup and repair services, ensuring that your instrument remains in peak playing condition. Whether you need a restringing, fretwork, or a complete restoration, our skilled technicians are here to help.

More Than Just a Store

Gid’s Guitars is more than just a retail shop—it’s a hub for the local music community. We regularly host workshops, clinics, and live performances to bring musicians together and foster creativity. From guitar maintenance classes to exclusive artist showcases, we strive to create an environment where players can learn, connect, and grow in their craft. As a locally owned business, we understand the importance of supporting the arts and nurturing talent. We work closely with schools, music programs, and local artists to encourage musical education and passion in future generations.

Visit Us

Whether you’re searching for your dream guitar, need expert advice, or simply want to be part of a thriving musical community, we invite you to visit Gid’s Guitars. Experience the difference that passion, knowledge, and exceptional service can make. Come in, pick up a guitar, and let your musical journey begin. Thank you for making Gid’s Guitars your trusted music destination for nearly five decades. We look forward to serving you for many more years to come!

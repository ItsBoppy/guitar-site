Fender Custom Shop 1959 250K Jazzmaster

The Fender Custom Shop 1959 250K Jazzmaster Journeyman Relic is a remarkable reincarnation of the classic model. This vintage guitar comes with a pair of hand-wound '62 single coils that are known for delivering a powerful, open tone often associated with Jazzmasters. These electronics ensure your music packs a serious punch, giving you the ability to bring your performances to life. Additionally, the vintage dual-circuit wiring and potentiometers offer superior control over your sound. For an enhanced playability, this innovative instrument is crafted with a rift-sawn maple neck, which increases stability and durability for a smooth playing experience. With its '62 C profile, you are provided with a comfortable grip and a natural feel, making it easier to play over extended periods.

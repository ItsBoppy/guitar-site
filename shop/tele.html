Vintage Fender 1956 Telecaster®

With the Fender 1956 Telecaster NOS Aged White Blonde, the Fender Custom Shop presents an authentic reissue of the original 56 Telecaster with a flawless NOS finish, which conveys the look and feel of a brand-new instrument from the golden era of the electric guitar. In addition to the two-piece body made of select lightweight ash wood, the electric guitar features a bolt-on flamed maple neck in the "V" profile typical of the mid-fifties. Two hand-wound Fender Custom Shop '55/'56 Telecaster single-coils also provide the typical Fifties twang on the guitar amplifier, which translates clean country pickings just as skilfully as classic rock riffs in the overdrive channel. Historically correct hardware with a typical Telecaster bridge and Vintage Style tuners complete the Fender 1956 Telecaster NOS Aged White Blonde.

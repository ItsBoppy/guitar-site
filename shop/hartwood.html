Hartwood Sonata Concert

Introducing the Hartwood Sonata concert electro-acoustic in bourbon-burst, designed with excellent ergonomics, versatile tonality and rich tonewoods. Comprised of a solid Sitka spruce top, flamed maple body and a C-shaped maple neck, the Sonata's premium selection of woods and meticulous build process guarantees a comfortable and responsive playing experience. The inbuilt Fishman Presys pre-amp provides the option to plug the guitar in to an amp or PA system, with excellent control over tone, volume and phase, plus an in-built LED tuner for pitch-perfect performances. And, to top off the finest of playing experiences, the Sonata concert comes equipped with D'addario's long lasting coated EXP16 strings, giving you a great sound straight out of the box.
